Solutions

Engineered for Precision

Comprehensive environmental control solutions designed for demanding applications.

Environmental Rooms

Custom controlled spaces for testing, storage, and process stability.

  • Stability chambers with precise temperature and humidity control
  • Cold storage rooms for pharmaceutical and biotech applications
  • Environmental test chambers for product validation
  • Walk-in freezers and coolers for industrial use
  • Custom-sized rooms built to your specifications

Custom Controls

Purpose-built control systems that maintain tight tolerances and clear visibility.

  • PLC-based control systems with HMI interfaces
  • Remote monitoring and alarm notification systems
  • Data logging and trending capabilities
  • Integration with building management systems (BMS)
  • 21 CFR Part 11 compliant solutions available

Conditioning Systems

Engineered conditioning solutions integrated for reliability and performance.

  • HVAC systems designed for precision environments
  • Humidity control with desiccant or refrigerant systems
  • Custom air handling units for specialized applications
  • Redundant systems for critical environments
  • Energy-efficient solutions with heat recovery options
